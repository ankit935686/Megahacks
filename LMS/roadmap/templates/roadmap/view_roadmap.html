Career Development Roadmap

Harsh Dalvi

1. CAREER PROGRESSION PATH

Short-term goals (next 1-2 years)

  • Secure a full-time internship in backend development at a reputable company.
  • Gain practical experience with a variety of backend technologies (e.g., expand beyond Django, explore Node.js or other frameworks).
  • Contribute to open-source projects to build a stronger portfolio and network.
  • Complete at least one relevant industry certification (see section 3).

Medium-term goals (3-5 years)

  • Become a proficient backend developer with expertise in at least two major frameworks (e.g., Django, Node.js).
  • Take on increasing responsibility within a team, potentially leading smaller projects.
  • Explore specialization within backend development (e.g., microservices, cloud technologies).
  • Consider pursuing a Master's degree (if desired) in a related field.

Long-term vision (5+ years)

  • Lead complex backend development projects, mentoring junior engineers.
  • Become a technical architect or senior engineer, influencing technological direction within a company.
  • Potentially start your own venture leveraging backend development skills.
2. SKILL DEVELOPMENT PLAN

Technical skills to acquire or improve

  • Databases: PostgreSQL, MongoDB (NoSQL experience)
  • Cloud platforms: AWS, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), or Azure
  • API development and integration: RESTful APIs, GraphQL
  • Containerization and orchestration: Docker, Kubernetes
  • Testing and debugging: Unit testing, integration testing, debugging tools
  • Advanced JavaScript frameworks: React, Angular or Vue.js (for potential full-stack capabilities)

Soft skills to develop

  • Communication: Effectively articulate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Teamwork and collaboration: Strengthen collaborative skills within agile development environments
